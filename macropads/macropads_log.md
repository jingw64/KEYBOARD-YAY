## ~~Macropad v0~~ Burgerpad

This is the first project we will be making in the keyboard/macropad series to help us get used to tools and the general process of everything.

---

### May 19th, 2026

Created the Git Repo, started the Macropad project, and imported the necessary libraries into KiCad.

We decided we're going to use this repository for the footprints because of its wide variety of switches:

[marbastlib footprint repo](https://github.com/ebastler/marbastlib?ref=ohmbedded.com)

**Next step: Decide on microcontroller**

---

### May 20th, 2026

Did some research into microcontrollers (what they are, which ones to use).

[Microcontroller Documentation](../knowledge/Microcontroller!.md)

Ended up deciding on the RP2040 due to its software compatibilities, size, and price. Imported its symbols and footprints into KiCad.

**Did not decide next step, left for a break and hopped back on at 12 am**

---

### May 21st, 2026

We realized we hadn't figured out what we wanted the keyboard layout to look like, in addition to the purpose of the macropad itself. 

Designed the layout in [keyboard layout editor](https://www.keyboard-layout-editor.com/) and settled on what we wanted to make a macropad for. Renamed project from macropad_v0 to burgerpad due to the burger-like shape of the layout.[^1]
