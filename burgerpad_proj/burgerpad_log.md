# Macropad

This is the first project of our keyboard series! Here's a log that details some of the work we did and our thought process behind it.

## Macropad v0

### May 19th, 2026

Created the Git Repo, started the Macropad project, and imported the necessary libraries into KiCad.

We decided we're going to use this repository for the footprints because of its wide variety of switches:

[marbastlib footprint repo](https://github.com/ebastler/marbastlib?ref=ohmbedded.com)

Next step: Decide on microcontroller

<br>

### May 20th, 2026

Did some research into microcontrollers (what they are, which ones to use). [Microcontroller Documentation](../knowledge/Microcontroller!.md)

Ended up deciding on the RP2040 due to its software compatibilities, size, and price. Imported its symbols and footprints into KiCad.
