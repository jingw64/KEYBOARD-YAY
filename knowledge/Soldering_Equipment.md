# Soldering Equipment!

Date: May 22, 2026

Authors: Richard Ding

## What we gotta buy

Since this is our first project, we have to buy some baseline soldering equipment. This includes:

- Soldering equipment:
  - Soldering iron, so that you can actually solder
  - Cleaning or brass sponge, to keep the tip clean
- Solder materials:
  - Solder, leaded or unleaded
  - Solder Flux, to make soldering a morbillion times easier
- Nice-to-haves:
  - Tweezers, for SMD components
  - Multimeter, for voltage and continuity checks
  - Spare tips, in case you mess up real bad
  - Fume hood, so you don’t breathe in lead and flux fumes (or just go outside if you don’t want to)
